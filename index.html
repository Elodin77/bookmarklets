Find out who unfollowed you on Instagram

ig unfollowers

Bookmark the link above (you can drag it to your bookmarks bar).
Go to your instagram profile and click followers, then click the bookmark and wait.
Go to your instagram profile and click following, then click the bookmark and wait.
Click here.
Use CTRL+A and CTRL+C to copy the entire output, and CTRL+V to paste the followers on the left, and the following on the right.
Highlighted on the left will show those following you who you aren't following.
Highlighted on the right will show those you are following who aren't following you.
